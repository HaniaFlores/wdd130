Cascada del Barro, Nuevo Leon

Beginner

  • Duration: 4 hrs
  • Start Hour: 6:30 am
  • End Hour: 10:30 am
  • Distance: 5.5 km / 3.2 miles
  • Max Height: 975 masl
  • Location: El Barro, Santiago, NL

Description

The Cascada del Barro is an unparalleled experience, it has incredible views and a friendly and simple path that allows you to enjoy a trail on a mountain with an imposing forest, next to a river that carries a lot of water in seasons. On the other hand, the experience allows you to reach a waterfall hidden in the forest which is unique and unforgettable, nature seems to be another ecosystem totally different from the one we usually see in the mountains. Special for beginners or people who are entering the world of hiking. Without a doubt, an experience that you cannot miss!

Las Minas, Nuevo Leon

Intermediate

  • Duration: 6 hrs
  • Start Hour: 6:00 am
  • End Hour: 12:00 pm
  • Distance: 8 km / 5 miles
  • Max Height: 2020 masl
  • Location: Monterrey, NL

Description

Las Minas is an incredible hike that has stunning views and a wonderful summit where you can see the entire city; in addition to seeing the sunrise on one side of Cerro de la Silla. We will enter a cave to get to know a little about the great mountain of Mitras. The tour is friendly and simple and has parts that will leave us speechless. It is a unique and ideal experience for those who enter the world of hiking. You can't miss it, see you in the mountains!
