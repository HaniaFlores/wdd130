Overview

Purpose

The website will increase the presence of the company on the internet and will make it easier for the clients to book an experience. Clients will have access to the price, and a brief explanation of the trips we offer and will be able to book one; the best part is the client can visit our website at any time.

Audience

Families who want to feel connected to nature or relax through hiking. Most clients will be young adults but everyone in the family can participate since we have hiking trials with different difficulty levels. Clients will access the website through laptops or any mobile device.

On the other hand, the experience allows you to reach a waterfall hidden in the forest which is unique and unforgettable, nature seems to be another ecosystem totally different from the one we usually see in the mountains. The tour has special easy parts for trekking and enjoying an incredible tour. Price: $45 Dates: January 15, 18, 26 February 3, 11, 19, 27 March 4

Las Minas, Nuevo Leon Level: Intermediate Duration: 6 hrs Start Hour: 6:00 am End Hour: 12:00 pm Distance: 8 km / 5 miles Max Height: 2020 masl Location: Monterrey, NL Las Minas is an incredible hike that has stunning views and a wonderful summit where you can see the entire city; in addition to seeing the sunrise on one side of Cerro de la Silla. FAQS

Frequently Asked Questions This is a place to answer frequently asked client questions.

How far in advance should I register for a trip? You can never register too early, but the ideal booking window is usually the 3–5-month time frame. Bottom line, if you know you want to do a trip go ahead and register as soon as you know! I've never hiked or backpacked before - is that a problem? No, prior experience backpacking or hiking is not required or expected to join any of our trips. What is important is that your physical fitness matches the difficulty of the trip you choose. What equipment or clothing is required to wear? At the time of making your purchase, you will receive a guide for what clothing, accessories, and equipment to bring to your experiences. What is included in the trip? • Guides trained in first aid. • First aid kit. • Snacks. • Hydration (We will provide a drink at the end of the experience. Hydration such as water and energy or moisturizing drinks is the responsibility of each person to carry in their backpack.)
